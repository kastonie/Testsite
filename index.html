Desk Duty

When Lily has had enough of resting and you've been sitting at your desk for too long, she takes matters into her own paws. Literally.

She'll walk over, size you up, and then — chomp. A firm but loving bite on the hand or arm. Not aggressive. Just a firm reminder that the couch isn't going to walk itself, and neither are you.

Consider it her version of a standing desk reminder. She's not attacking you — she's managing your work-life balance.
